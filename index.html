How I work

How I approach a problem.

  1. Start from lived pain. Every build began with a real frustration — three years of RFP grind, churn that quietly cost accounts, markets I was trading by hand.
  2. Model it with data. Turn the pain into something measurable — RAG retrieval, survival models, time-series — and validate it out-of-sample, not on a hunch.
  3. Ship a tool someone uses. Wrap it in a real product — Next.js, Supabase, Claude API — not a notebook that dies in a folder.
